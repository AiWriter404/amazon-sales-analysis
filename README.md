# 🛍️ Amazon Sales — Price & Rating Analysis

Exploratory Data Analysis of Amazon India products 
using Python. Includes price analysis, rating analysis, 
discount patterns, and category insights.

---

## 📊 Project Overview

Analyzed 1,462 Amazon products across 9 categories 
to understand pricing, discounts, and customer ratings.

---

## 🔍 What I Did

- Cleaned price columns by removing ₹ and , symbols
- Fixed data types for price, rating and discount
- Created new features like amount saved
- Built 6 professional visualizations

---

## 📈 Key Findings

- Average discount is 47.7% — almost half price!
- Average product rating is 4.1 out of 5
- Electronics is the top category with 520+ products
- OfficeProducts has the highest average rating
- AmazonBasics is the most reviewed brand
- Price and rating have no strong relation

---

## 🛠️ Tools Used

- Python
- Pandas
- Matplotlib
- Seaborn
- NumPy
